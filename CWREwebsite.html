About Us

Welcome to Columbia women ROCK entrepreneurship!

Columbia Women Rock Entrepreneurship (CWRE) is a student-led club on campus, aiming to bring female entrepreneurs' voices to campus and build role models for future female entrepreneurs.

Our Events

In the past year, CWRE held a series of keynote talks and workshops to Columbia students, and offered extensive networking opportunities with New York City female entrepreneurs.

Guest Speakers

Listen to true stories of experts in various industries. You will be inspired by their experiences and will have a better sense of what to expect in those industries.

Our Team

The members of Columbia Women Rock Entrepreneurship are a group of students who are interested in women empowerment and entrepreneurship. The purpose of the group is to provide opportunities to Columbia students to learn from women entrepreneurs and prepare them to enter entrepreneurship world by either starting their own startups or working for startups.
